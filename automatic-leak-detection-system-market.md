<p><p>The Automatic Leak Detection System market comprises two main types: Fully Automatic Leak Detection Systems and Semi-Automatic Leak Detection Systems. Fully Automatic systems operate independently to identify and alert users of leaks without human intervention, using advanced sensors and monitoring technologies. In contrast, Semi-Automatic systems require some level of human involvement for monitoring and interpretation of data, allowing operators to review alerts and make informed decisions. Both types enhance safety and efficiency in various industries, including oil and gas, water distribution, and manufacturing.</p></p>
