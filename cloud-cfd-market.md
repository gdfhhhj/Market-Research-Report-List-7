<p><strong>The Cloud CFD Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Steady Simulation</li><li>Unsteady Simulation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Cloud CFD market is categorized into two primary types: Steady Simulation and Unsteady Simulation. Steady Simulation focuses on time-invariant fluid flow, providing consistent solutions for scenarios with stable conditions, making it ideal for designs that don't vary over time. In contrast, Unsteady Simulation accounts for time-dependent changes in fluid dynamics, suitable for complex scenarios like turbulent flows or transient phenomena. Both types leverage cloud computing for scalable resources, enhancing simulation capabilities and efficiency in various engineering applications.</p></p>
