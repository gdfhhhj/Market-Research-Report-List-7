<p><p>A Combined Cycle Steam Turbine (CCST) is an advanced power generation technology that enhances overall efficiency by integrating gas and steam turbines. In a combined cycle system, the waste heat from the gas turbine is utilized to produce steam in a heat recovery steam generator (HRSG), which then powers the steam turbine. This dual approach maximizes energy output while minimizing fuel consumption, making it an environmentally sustainable choice for electricity generation.</p><p>The Combined Cycle Steam Turbine Market is experiencing notable growth as energy demands rise globally.
