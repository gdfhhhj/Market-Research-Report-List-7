<p><ul><li>Blown Films</li><li>Cast Films</li></ul></p>
<p><p>MDO-PE films, or Machine Direction Oriented Polyethylene films, are categorized into two main types: blown films and cast films. Blown films are produced by extruding molten polyethylene through a ring die, creating a tube that is inflated to form a thin film with excellent mechanical properties and barrier performance. In contrast, cast films are made by extruding the polymer into a flat sheet and then cooling it on a casting surface, resulting in a smoother, more uniform film ideal for various packaging applications.</p></p>
