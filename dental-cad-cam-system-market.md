<p><p>Dental CAD & CAM systems are advanced technologies used in dentistry for designing and manufacturing dental restorations, such as crowns, bridges, and dentures. Factors contributing to this robust growth include increasing dental procedures, a rise in cosmetic dentistry, and growing awareness of oral health among populations worldwide. Adoption of digital dentistry technologies further fuels market expansion, offering benefits like enhanced precision, reduced turnaround times, and improved patient outcomes. </p><p>Recent trends indicate a significant shift towards integrated solutions, where CAD & CAM systems are combined with other digital tools such as intraoral scanners for seamless workflows.
