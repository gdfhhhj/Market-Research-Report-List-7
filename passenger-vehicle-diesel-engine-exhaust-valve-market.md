<p><p>The passenger vehicle diesel engine exhaust valve market consists of three main types: mono metallic valves, bimetallic valves, and hollow valves. Mono metallic valves are made from a single material, offering durability and cost-effectiveness. Bimetallic valves combine two metals to enhance thermal resistance and performance. Hollow valves are lightweight and designed for high-performance applications, improving thermal efficiency. Each type caters to specific engine requirements, impacting overall vehicle performance, emissions, and longevity in diesel engine applications.</p></p>
