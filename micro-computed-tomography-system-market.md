<p><p>The micro-computed tomography (micro-CT) system market is segmented into Ex-Vivo and In-Vivo micro-CT scanning types. Ex-Vivo micro-CT scanning involves imaging samples outside of a living organism, allowing for detailed analysis of biological tissues, bones, and other specimens in a controlled environment. In contrast, In-Vivo micro-CT scanning captures three-dimensional images of living organisms in real-time, enabling researchers to study anatomical structures and physiological processes without invasive procedures. Both types serve critical roles in biological research and medical diagnostics.</p></p>
