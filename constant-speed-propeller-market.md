<p><p>The constant speed propeller market comprises primarily hydraulic and electric propellers. Hydraulic propellers utilize oil pressure to adjust blade angles, optimizing performance across various flight conditions. They are commonly found in larger aircraft due to their efficiency and reliability. In contrast, electric propellers employ electric motors for blade angle adjustments, offering precise control and reduced maintenance. This technology is gaining traction in smaller, more environmentally-friendly aircraft, highlighting the growing shift toward sustainable aviation solutions in the market.</p></p>
