<p><p>The Optical PVD (Physical Vapor Deposition) Coating Equipment market primarily comprises three types: Evaporation Coating, Ion Sputter Coating, and Others. </p><p>Evaporation Coating involves vaporizing materials in a vacuum to deposit thin films, ideal for producing optical coatings with high purity. </p><p>Ion Sputter Coating utilizes energetic ion bombardment to dislodge target atoms and deposit them onto substrates, ensuring enhanced adhesion and uniformity. </p><p>The "Others" segment includes alternative techniques such as atomic layer deposition and pulsed laser deposition, catering to specialized applications.</p></p>
