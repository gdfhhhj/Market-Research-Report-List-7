<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Handheld Laser Rangefinder Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Phase Method Rangefinder</li><li>Pulse Method Rangefinder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The handheld laser rangefinder market can be categorized into two main types: phase method rangefinders and pulse method rangefinders. Phase method rangefinders determine distance by measuring the phase shift of a laser beam reflected from an object, offering high accuracy and rapid measurements, making them ideal for close-range applications. In contrast, pulse method rangefinders emit short laser pulses and measure the time it takes for the pulse to return, enabling longer-range measurements, typically suitable for surveying and outdoor activities.</p></p>
