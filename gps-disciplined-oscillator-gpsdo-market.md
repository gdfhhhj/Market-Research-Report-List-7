<p><p>A GPS Disciplined Oscillator (GPSDO) is a precise timing device that employs GPS signals to stabilize and enhance the accuracy of an oscillator, typically quartz or rubidium. By utilizing GPS signals, GPSDOs can maintain highly accurate timekeeping even in the absence of satellite signals, reducing drift and improving overall reliability.</p><p>The GPSDO market is poised for significant growth, driven by the increasing demand for high-precision timing solutions across various sectors.
