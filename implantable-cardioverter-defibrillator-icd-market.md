<p><p>The Implantable Cardioverter Defibrillator (ICD) market includes several types designed to manage cardiac arrhythmias. Single Chamber ICDs are positioned in either the right atrium or ventricle, suitable for simple cases. Dual Chamber ICDs monitor and stimulate both chambers, improving heart rhythm coordination. Biventricular ICDs, often used in heart failure patients, engage both ventricles for synchronized contractions, enhancing cardiac output. Each type addresses specific patient needs, influencing market dynamics based on prevalence and treatment advancements.</p></p>
