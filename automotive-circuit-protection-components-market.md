<p><p>The **Automotive Circuit Protection Components Market** includes various types designed to safeguard vehicle electronics. **Automotive Blade Fuses** are widely used to protect circuits by breaking the connection during overcurrent. **Automotive Grade PTC Thermistors** limit current flow by increasing resistance when temperatures rise. **Automotive Grade TVS Diodes** protect against voltage spikes by diverting excess energy. **Polymeric ESD Suppressors** guard against electrostatic discharge, ensuring sensitive components remain safe. **Other** components include circuit breakers and varistors, offering additional protection against electrical faults in automotive systems.</p></p>
