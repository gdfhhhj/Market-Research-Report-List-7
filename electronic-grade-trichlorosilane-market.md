<p><p>The Electronic Grade Trichlorosilane market primarily consists of two production processes: the Direct Chlorination (DC) process and the Hydrochlorination (HC) process. The DC process involves the direct reaction of silicon with chlorine gas, producing high-purity trichlorosilane efficiently. In contrast, the HC process utilizes hydrogen chloride and silicon to achieve similar outcomes but may involve more steps and impurities. Both methods cater to the electronics industry, supplying essential materials for semiconductor manufacturing and solar cell production.</p></p>
