<p><p>The ventilation cooling fans market comprises several types, primarily axial fans, centrifugal fans, and others. Axial fans move air parallel to the fan's axis, making them efficient for low-pressure applications, ideal for cooling and exhaust. Centrifugal fans, on the other hand, draw air in at a right angle to the axis, generating higher pressure suitable for large spaces or ducted systems. Other types may include mixed-flow and blower fans, catering to various industrial needs and specialized applications.</p></p>
