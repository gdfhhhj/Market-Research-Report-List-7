<p><p>The 3D medical imaging equipment market encompasses various technologies crucial for diagnostics. X-ray systems provide quick imaging to identify fractures and infections. Ultrasound employs sound waves for real-time visualizations of soft tissues and fetal monitoring. MRI uses strong magnetic fields for detailed images of organs and structures, particularly soft tissues. CT scans combine X-ray measurements from different angles for cross-sectional images, enhancing diagnostic precision. Hybrid imaging merges technologies, like PET/CT, optimizing functional and anatomical insights for comprehensive patient assessments.</p></p>
