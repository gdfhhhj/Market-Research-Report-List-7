<p><p>Fire Fighting Foam is a specialized material used to extinguish fires, particularly those involving flammable liquids like oil, gasoline, and chemicals. It works by forming a blanket over the fuel surface, cutting off the fire's oxygen supply and cooling the flames. Fire fighting foams are categorized into classes such as AFFF (Aqueous Film Forming Foam), AR-AFFF (Alcohol-Resistant AFFF), protein-based, and synthetic foams, each designed for specific fire types.</p><p>The Fire Fighting Foam Market is experiencing significant growth, driven by increasing industrial safety regulations, rising demand from oil & gas, aviation, and manufacturing sectors, and growing awareness about fire safety. Additionally, the development of high-performance foams for challenging fire scenarios and the integration of IoT-based fire safety systems are shaping the industry. North America and Europe dominate the market, while Asia-Pacific is emerging as a high-growth region due to rapid industrialization and urbanization.</p></p>
