<p><p>Embedded Multimedia Card (eMMC) is a type of flash storage technology commonly used in smartphones, tablets, laptops, and other electronic devices. As consumers seek devices with enhanced storage capabilities for applications like gaming, streaming, and multimedia consumption, manufacturers are integrating higher capacity eMMC solutions into their products.
