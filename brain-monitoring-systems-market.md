<p><p>The brain monitoring systems market comprises various imaging and monitoring technologies used for assessing brain activity and function. CT (Computed Tomography) provides 3D imaging for detecting structural abnormalities. MRI (Magnetic Resonance Imaging) offers high-resolution images of brain tissues. EEG (Electroencephalography) measures electrical activity through scalp electrodes, while MEG (Magnetoencephalography) records magnetic fields produced by neural activity. fNIRS (functional Near-Infrared Spectroscopy) monitors blood flow changes associated with brain activity. Other methods may include PET scans and advanced wearable devices, expanding diagnostic and research capabilities.</p></p>
