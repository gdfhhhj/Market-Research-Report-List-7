<p><p>The flash dryers market is primarily categorized into direct and indirect drying types. Direct drying involves the exposure of materials to a hot gas stream, facilitating rapid moisture removal, making it ideal for heat-sensitive materials. In contrast, indirect drying employs surfaces heated externally, minimizing gas contact and preserving the integrity of delicate products. Both methods are widely used across industries such as food processing, chemicals, and pharmaceuticals, offering tailored solutions for efficient moisture management based on product requirements.</p></p>
