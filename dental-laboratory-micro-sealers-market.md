<p><p>The dental laboratory micro-sealers market encompasses various technologies, including laser, infrared, and pulse arc systems. Laser micro-sealers use focused light energy to create precise, strong seals on materials. Infrared micro-sealers operate by emitting infrared radiation, effectively bonding dental materials through heat. Pulse arc systems generate short bursts of electrical energy, producing rapid, high-temperature sealing. Each type offers unique advantages in terms of speed, efficiency, and bonding quality, catering to diverse dental laboratory needs for durable and reliable results.</p></p>
