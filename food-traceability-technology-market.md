<p><p>Food traceability technology includes various systems that enhance the tracking of food products from farm to table. Radio Frequency Identification (RFID) and Real Time Location Systems (RTLS) enable real-time monitoring of food items throughout the supply chain. Global Positioning System (GPS) provides location data for transportation tracking. Barcodes facilitate easy scanning and data retrieval at various points. Infrared sensors offer additional monitoring capabilities, while biometric systems use unique biological traits for secure identification, ensuring the integrity and safety of food products.</p></p>
