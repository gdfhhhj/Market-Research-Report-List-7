<p><p>Advanced CT machines are categorized based on the number of detector rows, which influences their imaging speed and resolution. **128-row CT machines** offer enhanced imaging compared to traditional models, suitable for routine scans. **256-row systems** provide faster scans and better 3D imaging capabilities, ideal for complex cases. **320-row machines** deliver high-speed imaging with improved coverage for cardiac and trauma patients. **640-row CT machines** excel in comprehensive imaging, allowing for extremely high-resolution images in a single rotation. Other types may include specialized or hybrid systems tailored for specific diagnostic needs.</p></p>
