<p><p>Molded fiber products are eco-friendly packaging materials made from recycled paperboard, newsprint, or other natural fibers. Additionally, advancements in manufacturing technologies have improved the durability and versatility of molded fiber products, further fueling their adoption.</p><p>Recent trends in the market include the development of molded fiber products for premium packaging, such as electronics and cosmetics, and the use of innovative designs to enhance product aesthetics.
