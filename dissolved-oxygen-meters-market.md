<p><p>The dissolved oxygen meters market is categorized into two main types: polarographic and primary battery type. Polarographic dissolved oxygen meters utilize a sensor that measures oxygen levels through electrochemical reactions, providing accurate and real-time readings, often suited for laboratory and industrial applications. In contrast, primary battery type dissolved oxygen meters are portable, using battery power to operate, offering convenience and ease of use in field settings. Both types play crucial roles in water quality monitoring across various sectors.</p></p>
