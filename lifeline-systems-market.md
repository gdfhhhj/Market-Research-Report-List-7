<p><strong>The Lifeline Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Vertical Lifeline</li><li>Horizontal Lifeline</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Lifeline systems are crucial for fall protection in various industries, categorized into vertical and horizontal lifelines. Vertical lifelines are typically anchored above the user, allowing them to move vertically while secured, often utilized in construction or maintenance. In contrast, horizontal lifelines are stretched between two or more anchor points, providing freedom of movement along a plane, commonly used in roofing, scaffolding, and industrial settings. Both systems ensure worker safety during tasks at elevation, adapting to specific environmental challenges.</p></p>
