<p><p>The eye tracking hardware market includes two main types: screen eye trackers and wearable eye trackers. Screen eye trackers are fixed devices that monitor users' eye movements while they interact with screens, often used in research and usability studies. In contrast, wearable eye trackers are portable devices, such as glasses, that allow for tracking eye movements in natural environments, offering greater mobility and real-world data collection. Both types serve various applications, including marketing research, gaming, and assistive technology.</p></p>
