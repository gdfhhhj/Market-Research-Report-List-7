<p><strong>The Gene Panel Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Amplicon-based Approach</li><li>Hybridization-based Approach</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The gene panel market can be categorized into amplicon-based and hybridization-based approaches. The amplicon-based approach utilizes PCR amplification to target specific gene regions, allowing for efficient sequencing of selected DNA segments. In contrast, the hybridization-based approach employs probes that bind to complementary DNA sequences, facilitating the detection and analysis of genetic variations. Both methods offer distinct advantages for genomic research and clinical diagnostics, catering to varying needs in precision medicine and variant detection within targeted gene panels.</p></p>
