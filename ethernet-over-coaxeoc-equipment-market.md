<p><p>The Ethernet over Coax (EoC) equipment market is generally divided into two main types: Passive EoC and Active EoC. </p><p>Passive EoC solutions utilize existing coaxial cables without requiring power for signal transmission, making them cost-effective and easy to install. In contrast, Active EoC systems use powered devices to amplify and boost the signal, providing extended range and better performance, especially in challenging environments. Both types serve to bridge the gap between traditional coaxial networks and modern Ethernet technologies.</p></p>
