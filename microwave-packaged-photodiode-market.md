<p><ul><li>AC Coupled</li><li>DC Coupled</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Microwave Packaged Photodiode market is segmented into AC Coupled and DC Coupled types. AC Coupled photodiodes filter out low-frequency components, making them suitable for applications involving modulation and alternating signals, as they emphasize signal integrity and bandwidth. In contrast, DC Coupled photodiodes maintain a continuous current flow, ideal for applications requiring precise measurements of light intensity. Both types are essential for diverse industries, including telecommunications, medical devices, and defense, based on their specific operational requirements.</p></p>
