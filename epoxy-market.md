<p><p>The epoxy market can be categorized into three main types: liquid, solid, and solution. Liquid epoxy is typically used for coatings and adhesives, offering excellent flow and curing properties. Solid epoxy usually comes in powder form and is ideal for applications requiring thick layers, such as for flooring and structural components. Solution epoxy involves a mixture dissolved in a solvent, providing versatile applications in various industries. Each type serves specific needs based on viscosity, application methods, and final performance characteristics.</p></p>
