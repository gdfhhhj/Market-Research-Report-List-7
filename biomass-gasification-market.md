<p><p>Biomass gasification is a process that converts organic materials, such as agricultural residues and wood, into synthesis gas (syngas) through high-temperature thermal decomposition in a controlled environment. This syngas, primarily composed of hydrogen, carbon monoxide, and carbon dioxide, can be used for electricity generation, heat production, and as a feedstock for chemicals and fuels. The growing emphasis on clean energy alternatives coupled with increasing waste management challenges is driving demand for biomass gasification technologies.</p><p>The Biomass Gasification Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 5.4% during the forecast period.
