<p><p>The deep-cycle battery market comprises two primary types: flooded deep-cycle batteries and sealed deep-cycle batteries. Flooded deep-cycle batteries utilize a liquid electrolyte and are known for their affordability and robustness, making them suitable for applications like renewable energy systems. Sealed deep-cycle batteries, including absorbed glass mat (AGM) and gel types, are maintenance-free, spill-proof, and offer better cycle life and safety, making them ideal for sensitive environments and portable applications. Each type serves distinct needs in energy storage solutions.</p></p>
