<p><p>The Quartz Facade market primarily comprises two main types: Press Molding and Casting Molding. Press Molding involves shaping quartz materials under high pressure to create durable and aesthetically appealing surfaces, commonly used in construction and interior design. Conversely, Casting Molding involves pouring a liquid quartz mixture into molds, allowing it to solidify into various shapes and finishes. Both methods offer distinct advantages in terms of customization, strength, and application, catering to diverse architectural and design needs.</p></p>
