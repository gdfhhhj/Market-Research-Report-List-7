<p><p>The DPC ceramic substrate market includes two primary types: alumina DPC ceramic substrates and aluminum nitride DPC ceramic substrates. Alumina substrates, known for their cost-effectiveness and good dielectric properties, are widely used in electronics for insulation and thermal management. Aluminum nitride substrates, on the other hand, offer superior thermal conductivity and are preferred in high-power and high-frequency applications, making them ideal for advanced electronic devices. Both types cater to diverse industries, enhancing performance and reliability in electronic systems.</p></p>
