<p><p>The thrombus management device market comprises several key types focusing on the treatment of blood clots. Neurovascular embolectomy devices are designed for removing clots in the brain’s blood vessels. Embolectomy balloon catheters facilitate clot retrieval by inflating at the clot site. Percutaneous thrombectomy devices mechanically extract clots through minimally invasive techniques. Catheter-guided thrombolysis devices deliver medications directly to dissolve clots. Together, these devices enhance patient outcomes by improving blood flow and reducing complications associated with thrombus formation.</p></p>
