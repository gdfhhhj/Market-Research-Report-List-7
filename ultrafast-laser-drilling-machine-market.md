<p><strong>The Ultrafast Laser Drilling Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Pulse</li><li>Multipulse</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ultrafast laser drilling machines operate using two primary market types: single pulse and multipulse systems. Single pulse machines deliver a high-energy pulse to create a precise hole in materials with minimal thermal damage, ideal for applications requiring fine detailing. In contrast, multipulse systems use a series of rapid pulses to gradually remove material, allowing for deeper or wider holes with enhanced control over the drilling process. Both types cater to various industrial needs, providing efficiency and precision in additive manufacturing and microfabrication.</p></p>
