<p><p>Oil-less scroll vacuum pumps are designed for efficient, quiet operation without the need for lubricants. The market features two main types: single-sided and double-sided scroll pumps. Single-sided scroll pumps use one stationary and one moving scroll to create a vacuum, ideal for low to medium vacuum applications. In contrast, double-sided scroll pumps have a pair of scrolls operating on both sides, allowing for higher pumping speeds and greater efficiency, making them suitable for demanding industrial processes and larger-scale applications.</p></p>
