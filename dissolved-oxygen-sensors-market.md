<p><strong>The Dissolved Oxygen Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Optical (Luminescent) Type</li><li>Clark Electrochemical (Amperometric) Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dissolved oxygen sensors market features two primary types: Optical (Luminescent) and Clark Electrochemical (Amperometric). Optical sensors utilize luminescent technology to measure dissolved oxygen through light emission and decay, offering high sensitivity and minimal interference. In contrast, Clark Electrochemical sensors operate based on amperometric principles, where an electrical current is generated in response to oxygen diffusion through a membrane, providing reliable measurements. Both types serve diverse applications in environmental monitoring, aquaculture, and industrial processes, fulfilling essential measurement needs.</p></p>
