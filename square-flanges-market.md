<p><p>The square flanges market can be categorized into three primary types: SHAB, SHB, and LSA. SHAB flanges, designed for high-pressure applications, feature a robust construction ideal for severe environments. SHB flanges, often used in general purpose settings, balance strength and weight, making them versatile. LSA flanges, distinguished by their lightweight design, are tailored for applications where reducing weight is crucial, such as in aerospace or automotive industries. Each type serves specific functional requirements, enhancing system efficiency.</p></p>
