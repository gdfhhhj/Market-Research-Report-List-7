<p><strong>The Wire-Cut Electrical Discharge Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wire-Cut Flushing Type</li><li>Wire-Cut Submerge Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wire-cut electrical discharge machine market is segmented into two main types: wire-cut flushing type and wire-cut submerge type. The wire-cut flushing type employs a continuous flow of dielectric fluid to remove debris during the machining process, enhancing efficiency and precision. In contrast, the wire-cut submerge type fully immerses the workpiece in dielectric fluid, providing superior cooling and better surface finish but often at a slower rate. Each type serves distinct manufacturing needs based on precision, speed, and application requirements.</p></p>
