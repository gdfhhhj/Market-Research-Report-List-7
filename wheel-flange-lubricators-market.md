<p><p>Wheel flange lubricators are systems designed to reduce friction and wear between train wheels and rails by applying lubricant to the wheel flanges. The **Single Line Solution** involves a single lubrication line that delivers lubricant to one side of the wheel flange, suitable for simpler applications. The **Double Line Solution** uses two lubrication lines, providing more precise and even lubrication on both sides of the flange, ideal for complex or high-performance rail systems requiring enhanced efficiency and reduced wear.</p></p>
