<p><p>Cardiac MRI testing systems are categorized into open and closed MRI systems. Open MRI systems feature a larger, more accessible design, providing comfort for patients with anxiety or claustrophobia, often at the expense of higher spatial resolution and imaging quality. In contrast, closed MRI systems utilize a traditional tube-shaped design, delivering superior image clarity and detail, essential for accurate cardiac diagnostics. The choice between these systems largely depends on patient needs, diagnostic requirements, and the healthcare facility's capabilities.</p></p>
