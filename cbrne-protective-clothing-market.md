<p><p>The CBRNe protective clothing market is categorized into gas-tight and gas-permeable types. Gas-tight clothing offers a complete seal against hazardous agents, preventing any penetration of gases, vapors, or liquids, making it suitable for high-risk environments. In contrast, gas-permeable clothing allows for breathability while still providing a degree of protection against chemical agents, enabling comfort and reducing heat stress in lower-risk scenarios. Both types serve essential roles in safeguarding personnel in CBRNe situations.</p></p>
