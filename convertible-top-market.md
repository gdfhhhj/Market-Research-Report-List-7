<p><ul><li>Hard Top</li><li>Soft Top</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The convertible top market includes two main types: hard tops and soft tops. Hard tops are made from rigid materials like fiberglass or metal, offering durability, better insulation, and a sleek, coupe-like appearance when closed. Soft tops are constructed from flexible materials such as vinyl or fabric, providing a classic convertible look, lighter weight, and easier storage. Hard tops are often preferred for their sturdiness and noise reduction, while soft tops are favored for their affordability and traditional aesthetic.</p></p>
