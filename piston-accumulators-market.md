<p><p>Piston accumulators are hydraulic devices designed to store energy in the form of pressurized fluid, which can be released when needed to assist in various hydraulic systems. These accumulators consist of a cylinder containing a piston that separates the hydraulic fluid from a gas chamber, allowing for efficient energy storage and release. Their applications span across several industries, including automotive, aerospace, construction, and manufacturing, where they enhance performance, improve efficiency, and provide surge protection.</p><p>The Piston Accumulators Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 10.4% during the forecast period, driven by increasing demand for energy efficiency and sustainable technologies.
