<p><strong>The Total Nitrogen & Sulfur Analyzer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Carried Out by the CLD Method</li><li>Carried Out by the Dumas Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Total Nitrogen and Sulfur Analyzer market features two primary methodologies: the Chemiluminescence Detection (CLD) method and the Dumas method. The CLD method involves oxidizing nitrogen compounds to nitrogen oxides, which are then measured using chemiluminescence, offering high sensitivity for trace nitrogen detection. Conversely, the Dumas method uses high-temperature combustion to convert nitrogen and sulfur into gaseous forms, allowing for direct quantification. These technologies cater to various industries, including agriculture, environmental monitoring, and food safety, each with unique analytical requirements.</p></p>
