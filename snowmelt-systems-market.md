<p><p>Snowmelt systems are designed to eliminate snow and ice from surfaces, enhancing safety and accessibility. Hydronic heating systems circulate heated water through tubing embedded in driveways or walkways, using energy efficiently to melt snow. In contrast, electric snowmelt systems employ electric cables or mats to generate heat directly, providing precise control but often at a higher operational cost. Both types cater to residential and commercial applications, ensuring winter conditions are managed effectively for improved convenience and safety.</p></p>
