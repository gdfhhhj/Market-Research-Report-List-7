<p><p>All-Wheel Drive (AWD) motorcycles are designed to deliver power to both the front and rear wheels, enhancing traction and stability, especially in off-road or challenging terrains. These motorcycles are equipped with either disc or drum braking systems. Disc braking systems use rotors and calipers for efficient, high-performance stopping power, ideal for high-speed or heavy-duty use. Drum braking systems, on the other hand, rely on internal shoes and are simpler, cost-effective, and suitable for lighter or less demanding applications. Both systems cater to diverse market needs in AWD motorcycles.</p></p>
