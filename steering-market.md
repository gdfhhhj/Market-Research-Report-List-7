<p><ul><li>Electro-Hydraulic Power Steering (EHPS)</li><li>Hydraulic Power Steering (HPS)</li><li>Manual</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Steering market types include Hydraulic Power Steering (HPS), Electro-Hydraulic Power Steering (EHPS), and Manual Steering. HPS utilizes hydraulic fluid to assist steering, providing a smooth and responsive feel, commonly used in older vehicles. EHPS combines hydraulic assistance with electric control for enhanced efficiency and adaptability, often found in modern vehicles with advanced features. Manual steering relies solely on mechanical linkages, offering simplicity and lower weight but requiring more effort to maneuver, primarily seen in basic or performance-focused cars.</p></p>
