<p><p>The semiconductor packaging and testing equipment market includes several key types: </p><p>1. **Prober**: Devices that test semiconductor wafers before packaging.</p><p>2. **Bonder**: Equipment that attaches semiconductor chips to substrates, ensuring electrical connections.</p><p>3. **Dicing Machine**: Cuts wafers into individual chips for packaging.</p><p>4. **Sorter**: Classifies chips based on performance and specifications after dicing.</p><p>5. **Handler**: Manages the transfer of chips between various stages of testing and packaging.</p><p>6. **Others**: Includes ancillary equipment and specialized tools used in semiconductor packaging processes.</p></p>
