<p><p>The vehicle-mounted spotlights market is categorized into four main types: HID, incandescent, LED, and halogen lamps. HID (High-Intensity Discharge) offers bright illumination and energy efficiency, making it popular for off-road and emergency use. Incandescent lights provide a warm glow but are less energy-efficient and have shorter lifespans. LED lights are increasingly favored for their longevity, low power consumption, and vibrant brightness. Halogen lamps, a variant of incandescent, offer better efficiency and brightness but still fall behind LEDs in overall performance.</p></p>
