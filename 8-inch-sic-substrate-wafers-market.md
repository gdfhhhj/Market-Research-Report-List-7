<p><p>The 8-inch silicon carbide (SiC) substrate wafers market can be categorized into two main types: conductive and semi-insulating. Conductive type wafers are designed for applications requiring low resistance, ideal for power electronics and high-frequency devices. In contrast, semi-insulating type wafers have high resistivity, making them suitable for RF and microwave applications, as well as for high-voltage devices. Both types are integral to advancing technologies in electric vehicles, renewable energy, and telecommunication industries, contributing to enhanced performance and efficiency.</p></p>
