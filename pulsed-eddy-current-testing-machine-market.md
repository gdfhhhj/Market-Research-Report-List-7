<p><p>Pulsed Eddy Current Testing Machines are categorized into two main types: Type I and Type II. Type I machines focus on detecting surface and near-surface defects in conductive materials, leveraging pulsed eddy currents for effective assessment. These are typically used in applications such as aerospace and automotive industries. Type II machines, on the other hand, offer enhanced depth penetration capabilities, allowing for the detection of sub-surface defects. This type is crucial for industries that require thorough integrity assessments, such as oil and gas.</p></p>
