<p><p>COVID-19 antibody rapid test kits are categorized primarily into IgG and IgM tests, along with other types. The IgG test detects past infections by identifying antibodies formed after recovery, indicating potential immunity. The IgM test, on the other hand, identifies recent infections, as these antibodies appear shortly after exposure. Other types may include combination tests or different antibody assays that provide additional information. These tests facilitate quick screening and help in understanding population immunity levels.</p></p>
