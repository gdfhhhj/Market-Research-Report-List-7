<p><ul><li>Polyester</li><li>Fluoropolymer</li><li>Siliconized Polyester</li><li>Plastisol</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The coated steel market features various types: Polyester, Fluoropolymer, Siliconized Polyester, and Plastisol. Polyester coatings offer durability and corrosion resistance, ideal for general applications. Fluoropolymer coatings provide excellent chemical resistance and UV stability, making them suitable for harsh environments. Siliconized Polyester combines the benefits of industry-grade finishes with enhanced weathering resistance. Plastisol, a PVC-based coating, offers flexibility and impact resistance, commonly used in appliances and outdoor products. Each type caters to specific needs across diverse industries.</p></p>
