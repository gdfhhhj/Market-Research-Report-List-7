<p><ul><li>Lateral Chip LED</li><li>Vertical Chip LED</li><li>Flip Chip LED</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Mini-LED chips market comprises three main types: Lateral Chip LED, Vertical Chip LED, and Flip Chip LED. Lateral Chip LEDs have a traditional side-emitting design, offering uniform light distribution, while Vertical Chip LEDs provide enhanced thermal management and efficiency by positioning the chip vertically. Flip Chip LEDs utilize a reverse mounting technique, improving electrical contact and heat dissipation, leading to better performance. Each type caters to specific applications, promoting advancements in display technology, especially in high-resolution screens and enhanced backlighting.</p></p>
