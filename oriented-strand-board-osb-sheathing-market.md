<p><p>Oriented Strand Board (OSB) sheathing is categorized into four primary types based on structural performance and moisture resistance. OSB/1 is light-duty board for internal applications with minimal moisture exposure. OSB/2 is suitable for load-bearing applications in dry conditions. OSB/3 is designed for structural use in humid environments, offering moisture resistance, while OSB/4 provides enhanced strength and water resistance for heavy-duty applications in extreme conditions. Each type serves specific construction needs, ensuring structural integrity and durability.</p></p>
