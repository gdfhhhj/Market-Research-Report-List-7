<p><p>The UPS battery packs market consists of two primary types: internal and external battery packs. Internal battery packs are integrated within the UPS unit, providing compact, space-saving solutions ideal for small-scale applications. They offer simplicity in installation and maintenance. In contrast, external battery packs are separate units that can be added to enhance runtime and capacity. These are suitable for larger systems where extended backup power is crucial, allowing for flexibility in scaling and accommodating specific power needs.</p></p>
