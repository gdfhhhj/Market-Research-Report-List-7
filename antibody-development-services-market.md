<p><ul><li>Monoclonal Antibody Development</li><li>Polyclonal Antibody Development</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The antibody development services market includes two primary types: monoclonal and polyclonal antibody development. Monoclonal antibody development focuses on creating identical immune cells that target a specific antigen, offering precision and consistency, widely utilized in therapies and diagnostics. In contrast, polyclonal antibody development involves multiple immune cell types that produce antibodies against various epitopes on an antigen, providing broader reactivity. Both types are crucial in research, therapeutics, and diagnostics, catering to diverse scientific and clinical applications.</p></p>
