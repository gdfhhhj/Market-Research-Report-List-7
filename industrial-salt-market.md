<p><p>The industrial salt market comprises three main types: sea salt, well and rock salt, and lake salt. Sea salt is harvested from evaporated seawater and is often used in food and industrial processes. Well and rock salt is mined from underground deposits, offering high purity for industrial applications. Lake salt is obtained from salt lakes through evaporation, commonly used in de-icing and chemical production. Each type serves distinct industries, highlighting their relevance in various industrial applications.</p></p>
