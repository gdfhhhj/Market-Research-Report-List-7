<p><p>The Tungsten CMP (Chemical Mechanical Planarization) slurry market is divided into two main types: selective tungsten slurries and non-selective tungsten slurries. Selective tungsten slurries are designed to target tungsten materials specifically, facilitating precise removal while protecting other substrates, making them ideal for advanced semiconductor applications. In contrast, non-selective tungsten slurries are effective on multiple materials, offering a more generalized approach to planarization. Both types serve crucial roles in semiconductor manufacturing, ensuring optimal surface quality and device performance.</p></p>
