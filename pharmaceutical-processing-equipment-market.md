<p><p>The pharmaceutical processing equipment market encompasses various types tailored for specific functions. Agitators mix ingredients effectively, while blowers facilitate drying and material handling. Capsule equipment is essential for filling and sealing dosage forms. Centrifuges separate substances based on density, aiding in purification processes. Dryers remove moisture from products, ensuring stability and longevity. Granulators transform powdered materials into granules for better handling and processing. Other equipment includes filters, conveyors, and packaging machines, all crucial for efficient pharmaceutical production.</p></p>
