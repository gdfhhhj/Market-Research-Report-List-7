<p><p>Microchannel coolers for electronics devices are designed to efficiently dissipate heat, essential for maintaining performance and reliability. The stackable type features multiple layers or "stacks" of microchannels, allowing for increased cooling capacity while minimizing space requirements. In contrast, the array type employs a grid-like configuration of microchannels, optimizing heat transfer across a larger surface area. Both types enhance thermal management, making them suitable for high-performance electronics, including data centers, power electronics, and advanced computing systems.</p></p>
