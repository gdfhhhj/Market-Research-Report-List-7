<p><p>Industrial machine vision lenses are categorized based on their mounting types, each designed to suit specific applications. C-Mount lenses are versatile and widely used, featuring a standard thread size. CS-Mount lenses are similar but with a shorter flange distance, ideal for compact setups. F-Mount lenses, commonly used in photography, offer high-quality imaging. S-Mount lenses are smaller, catering to miniaturized cameras. Other lenses include various proprietary mounts, each tailored for unique industrial requirements, enhancing image capture and processing capabilities.</p></p>
