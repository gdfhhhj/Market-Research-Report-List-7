<p><p>The flavored cheese market encompasses two primary types: natural cheese and processed cheese. Natural cheese is made from milk and incorporates natural flavors, often derived from herbs, spices, or other ingredients, enhancing its taste without additives. In contrast, processed cheese is manufactured by blending natural cheese with emulsifiers, stabilization ingredients, and flavorings, resulting in a smooth, uniform texture. Both types cater to diverse consumer preferences, offering unique culinary applications in snacks, cooking, and gourmet dishes.</p></p>
